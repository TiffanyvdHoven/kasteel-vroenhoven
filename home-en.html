Exterior

Castle Vroenhoven is fully renovated in 2005. The castle features a beautiful landscaped park garden with ponds, orchards, woods, impressive driveways and a wine cellar.

The location of the castle is particularly favorable. With good accessibility to motorways, cities such as Diest and Hasselt are just around the corner.

Farmhouse

The large farmhouse next to the castle on the estate, has also been completely renovated. The farmhouse features 5 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms and a kitchen.

Next to the farmhouse there are 2 large barns which could be transformed into stables.

Garden

The spacious terrace at the backside of the castle with fountain-pond gives an impressive and wide view over the estate.

The castle is on an estate of no less than 25 hectares with an optional approx. 45 hectares to purchase.
